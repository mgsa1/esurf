DEV LOG

A tunable ocean surface you can surf. Design a wave, then ride it.

Target feel: SSX3 meets Dolphin Olympics. Fluid physics, expressive movement, and waves worth exploring.

Wave simulator

  • Slider changes should ripple outward from the origin, not snap the whole surface at once
  • Blend between wave states so edits feel like shaping water, not swapping textures
  • Interference between wave layers — let complex shapes emerge from simple components
  • Support more wave types beyond Gerstner (plunging, spilling, reef break profiles)

Game feel

  • Carving should feel like cutting, not steering — tight edge response, wide arcs at speed
  • Pump timing that rewards rhythm — compress downhill, extend uphill, build real speed
  • Air that flows from the approach — launch angle, speed, and face steepness all matter
  • Landing quality: come in flat and keep speed, come in sideways and eat it
  • The wave gives you energy — skill decides what you do with it

Next up

  • Tricks: spins, grabs, combos — earned by the line, not a button press
  • Scoring: air height, lip launches, pump chains, clean landings
  • Wave presets as levels — gentle rollers to heavy reef breaks
  • Ghost replays — race your best line
  • Foam, spray, wake trails — make speed visible
  • Custom wave editor — draw your own equation
